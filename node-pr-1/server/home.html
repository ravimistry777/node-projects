Welcome to Animal World

Lion

The lion is known as the king of the jungle. It lives in groups called prides. These majestic creatures are symbols of strength and courage across many cultures.

Elephant

Elephants are the largest land animals and are very intelligent. They are known for their long trunks, large ears, and incredible memory.

Tiger

The tiger (Panthera tigris) is a large cat and a member of the genus Panthera native to Asia. It has a powerful, muscular body with a large head and paws.

Gorilla

Gorillas are large, primarily herbivorous,[1] great apes that live in the tropical forests of equatorial Africa. The genus Gorilla is divided into two species: the eastern gorilla and the western gorilla, and either four or five subspecies.

Panda

Pandas, or giant pandas, are iconic black-and-white bears from China's mountain forests, famous for their bamboo diet, which they eat for up to 12 hours daily using a special wrist bone that acts as a thumb.

Cheetah

The cheetah (Acinonyx jubatus) is the world's fastest land animal, built for speed with a slender body, long legs, flexible spine, and non-retractable claws for traction, reaching 60-70 mph (97-112 km/h) in short bursts.
